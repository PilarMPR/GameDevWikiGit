# Free-to-Play (F2P) Design

Designing **live-service, free-to-play** games, from
[[../sources/s017-f2p-design-handbook|the F2P Handbook]] (s017, Part 1 of 4).

## Core stance
- **Foundation of fun first.** Core design (fun mechanics, intuitive **3Cs**, engaging systems,
  onboarding, feedback loops, progression, accessibility) is the **bedrock**; *without it, no
  economy, retention loop, or monetization can survive* (s017, Conclusion).
- The driving question: build an experience **compelling enough that players return again and
  again** — i.e. retention is designed in from the core, not bolted on (s017).
- Explicitly built on **[[mda-framework|MDA]]** and **player-centric** design (s017 references).
