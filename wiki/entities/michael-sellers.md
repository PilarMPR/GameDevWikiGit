# Michael Sellers

Game designer, entrepreneur, and educator; author of **[[../sources/s001-advanced-game-design|Advanced Game Design: A Systems Approach]]** (s001).

- Frames games as **systems** and game design as **system design** — the central thesis of his book (s001).
- Background spans industry game development and academia (teaches game design); the book grew out of his teaching and industry experience (s001, Intro).
