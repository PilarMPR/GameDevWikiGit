# Design Reference — Tips, Lenses & Questions

Quick-reference sheets for every category, compiled from all 17 sources. Each page lists:
- **Schell's Lenses** applicable to the category (with key design questions)
- **Tips and principles** from Adams, Kremers, Hourences, Norman, Swink, Sellers, Fullerton, Macklin & Sharp, Hodent, and Koster

Use these during design review, whiteboarding, and playtesting analysis.
