## What it's about
A broad, structured treatment of **level design** spanning teaching the player, emotional
feedback, environments, story, and gameplay construction — more theory-forward than s003.

## Structure (six parts)
- **I. Introduction to Level Design** — game design vs level design; **teaching mechanisms**;
  level-design **goals & hierarchies**; **structure & methodology**; single- vs multiplayer;
  toward a **shared grammar** for level design (ch.1–6).
- **II. Emotional Feedback Systems** — **reward systems** (ch.7), **immersion** (ch.8),
  **negative emotions** (ch.9). A distinctive emphasis: levels deliberately orchestrate emotion.
- **III. Game Environments** — **visual experiences** (ch.10), **audio design** (ch.11).
- **IV. Game Stories** — **story & narrative** (ch.12).
- **V. Designing Gameplay** — **world building** (ch.13), **puzzles** (ch.14), **AI** (ch.15),
  **challenge** (ch.16).
- **VI. Final Thoughts** — considering the future; recommended reading; glossary.

## Defining ideas
- Levels as a **teaching apparatus** — introducing and reinforcing mechanics (s012, ch.2).
- **Emotional feedback systems** (reward / immersion / negative emotion) as a core lens (s012, Part II).
- A push toward a **shared grammar** for the discipline (s012, ch.6).
