# Lens #81 — Character Transformation

The protagonist should change meaningfully over the course of the story. Transformation that is earned through specific trials resonates deeply. A character who ends the same as they began has not completed a narrative arc. (s005, ch.18)

## Core Questions

> Does the protagonist change? Is the change meaningful and earned? Are there specific moments that cause the transformation?
