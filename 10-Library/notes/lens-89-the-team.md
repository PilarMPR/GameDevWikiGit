# Lens #89 — The Team

Game teams are systems too. Team alignment, shared vision, and clear communication directly determine design quality. (s005, ch.23)

## Core Questions

> Is the team aligned on the vision? Does everyone feel ownership? Are there communication breakdowns? Is the team's skillset appropriate for the game?
