# Lens #67 — Simplicity and Transcendence

Good game worlds are simpler than reality in interesting ways AND more wonderful than reality in interesting ways. Games occupy a unique space between the mundane and the magical. (s005, ch.15)

## Core Questions

> In what ways is my world simpler than the real world? In what ways is it more wonderful? How can I find the right balance?
