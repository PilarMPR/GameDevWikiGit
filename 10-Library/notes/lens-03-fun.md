# Lens #3 — Fun

Fun = pleasure with surprises. Not all parts of a game are equally fun; identifying and fixing the un-fun parts is the ongoing work of design. (s005, ch.3)

## Core Questions

> What parts of my game are fun, and why? What parts are not fun? What would happen if I removed them?
