# Lens #52 — The Puzzle

A good puzzle fits naturally into the world and its solution feels earned — logically derivable, not a guess. Integrated puzzles maintain immersion; explicit puzzles risk feeling tacked-on. (s005, ch.12)

## Core Questions

> Does the puzzle fit naturally into the game world? Does solving it feel satisfying and earned? Is the solution logically derivable from available information?
