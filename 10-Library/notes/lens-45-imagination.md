# Lens #45 — Imagination

Players' imaginations fill in gaps — often better than explicit detail. Know what to show and what to leave to imagination. The binoculars effect: a close-up first establishes; imagination sustains. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> What must the player understand that I need to make explicit? What can I leave to imagination? Which details are better imagined than shown?
