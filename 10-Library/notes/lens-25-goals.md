# Lens #25 — Goals

Clear goals are a prerequisite for flow. Good goals are: concrete (you know when you've achieved them), achievable (not impossible), worthwhile (they matter to the player). (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> Are the goals of my game clear? Do short-term goals support long-term? Do player goals align with designer goals? Do the goals feel worthwhile?
