# Raph Koster

Game designer, entrepreneur, author. Known for Ultima Online, Star Wars Galaxies. Author of *A Theory of Fun for Game Design* (s016).

## Key ideas

- **Fun = learning** — fun is the pleasurable signal from successfully acquiring a new pattern; "learning is the drug" (s016).
- **Grokking** — deepest pattern acquisition: complete internalization. When a game's patterns are grokked, boredom is inevitable.
- **Boredom as failure signal** — boredom means no new patterns are available.
- **Games as teachers** — games are formal systems stripped of real-world noise, designed to teach patterns.
- **Learnable middle = flow channel** — identical zone; different mechanistic vs. phenomenological framing.
