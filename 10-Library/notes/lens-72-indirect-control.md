# Lens #72 — Indirect Control

Six methods for guiding players without removing their sense of agency: constraints, goals, interface, visual design, characters, and music. When all six agree, guidance is irresistible. (s005, ch.16)

## Core Questions

> What indirect control methods am I using? Are they working without feeling manipulative? Are there opportunities to use more or different indirect controls?
