# Lens #66 — Obstacle

Good stories need good obstacles — ones that are interesting in their own right, not just blocking the protagonist's path. The obstacle reveals character and creates the conflict that drives narrative. (s005, ch.15)

## Core Questions

> Does the protagonist have a clear goal? Is the obstacle interesting in its own right? Does overcoming the obstacle produce character transformation?
