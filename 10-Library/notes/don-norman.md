# Don Norman

Designer, cognitive scientist, author. Director Emeritus of the Design Lab at UC San Diego. Author of *The Design of Everyday Things* (s015).

## Key ideas

- **Human-centered design (HCD)** — design must fit the cognitive capabilities and limitations of users (s015).
- **Six principles** — affordances, signifiers, constraints, mappings, feedback, conceptual models (s015).
- **Gulfs of execution and evaluation** — the two cognitive gaps designers must bridge (s015).
- **Signifiers vs. affordances** — affordances are relationships that enable action; signifiers communicate where/how to act (s015).
