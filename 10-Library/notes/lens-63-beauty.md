# Lens #63 — Beauty

Beautiful game worlds invite exploration and emotional investment. Beauty should serve the gameplay — a breathtaking view that also provides tactical information serves two purposes. (s005, ch.14)

## Core Questions

> Is the game world beautiful? Does that beauty serve the gameplay? Are there elements I could make more beautiful that would enhance the experience?
