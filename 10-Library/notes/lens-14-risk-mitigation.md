# Lens #14 — Risk Mitigation

Prototype to mitigate the greatest risks first, not to polish what you already know works. Test the riskiest elements — the ones that could kill the project — as early as possible when change is cheap. (s005, ch.7)

## Core Questions

> What could go wrong? Which risks are highest-impact? How can I test the riskiest elements first, while change is still cheap?
