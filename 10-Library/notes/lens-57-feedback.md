# Lens #57 — Feedback

Every player action must produce clear, immediate, informative feedback. Without it, meaningful play is impossible — players cannot know whether their actions mattered. (s005, ch.13)

## Core Questions

> Is the feedback for every player action clear, immediate, and informative? Does feedback correctly convey what happened and why?
