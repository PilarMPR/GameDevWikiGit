# Lens #92 — Technology

Technology is an element of the tetrad, not a separate concern. The right technology enables the design; the wrong technology constrains it unnecessarily. Identify technology risks early. (s005, ch.26)

## Core Questions

> Is the technology right for this game? Are there technology risks? Can the technology do what the design requires?
