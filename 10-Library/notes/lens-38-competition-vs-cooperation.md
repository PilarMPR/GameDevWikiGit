# Lens #38 — Competition vs. Cooperation

Mixed conflict (competing while cooperating) is the most socially rich and hardest to balance. The Joust example: players compete for points but can cooperate to survive longer. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Is the balance between competition and cooperation appropriate for my audience? Can I add more of one without sacrificing the other?
