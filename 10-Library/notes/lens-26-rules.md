# Lens #26 — Rules

Rules define the game's possibility space. Good rules are complete (cover all cases), understandable (players can learn them), and interesting (produce complex and varied situations from simple statements). (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> Are my rules understandable? Are there ambiguous rules? Are there loopholes that undermine the experience? Are rules as complete as they need to be?
