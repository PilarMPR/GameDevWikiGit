# Lens #33 — Triangularity

The most compelling choice: safe low-reward vs. risky high-reward. When calibrated correctly, neither is trivially dominant and the right answer depends on context. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Is there a low-risk, low-reward choice and a high-risk, high-reward choice? Is the gradient interesting? Are players ever tempted to take the risk?
