# Johan Huizinga & Roger Caillois

Play theorists. Huizinga (*Homo Ludens*, 1938): play is older than culture itself; coined "magic circle." Caillois (*Man, Play, and Games*, 1958): four play types (Agon, Alea, Mimicry, Ilinx) and two poles (Paidia vs. Ludus).

## Key ideas

- **Huizinga:** play is voluntary, separate, uncertain, unproductive, rule-governed (s004). Foundation for all subsequent game theory.
- **Caillois:** extended Huizinga with four play categories and the Paidia/Ludus spectrum (s013 via s004).
- **Magic circle** — bounded space where special rules obtain; extended by Salen & Zimmerman as a design concept.
