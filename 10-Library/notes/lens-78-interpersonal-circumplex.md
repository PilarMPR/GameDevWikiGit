# Lens #78 — Interpersonal Circumplex

Characters should cover different positions on the dominant/friendly axes. An all-dominant or all-friendly cast lacks social drama. Social tensions and alliances emerge from character position combinations. (s005, ch.18)

## Core Questions

> Do my characters span different positions in the dominant/submissive and friendly/hostile space? Are there interesting social dynamics between them?
