# Lens #37 — Cooperation

Cooperation fulfills belonging and relatedness needs that competition cannot. Design for genuine cooperation: success should require it, not just allow it. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Does my game support cooperation? Does it emerge naturally? Are there mechanics that require cooperation, or just permit it? How does it feel to cooperate successfully?
