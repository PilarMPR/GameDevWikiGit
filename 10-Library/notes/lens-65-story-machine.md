# Lens #65 — Story Machine

Games that generate interesting stories from their mechanics alone are story machines. The best story machines produce stories interesting not just to the player but to spectators. (s005, ch.15)

## Core Questions

> Does my game generate interesting stories by itself? Are the generated stories interesting to spectators? How can the game generate better stories?
