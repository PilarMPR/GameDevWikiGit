# Lens #13 — Eight Filters

A finished design must pass eight filters: artistic impulse, demographics, experience design, innovation, business/marketing, engineering, social/community, and playtesting. Changing to pass one may break another. (s005, ch.7)

## Core Questions

> Does the design pass all 8 filters? Which filters does it currently fail? Changing it to pass one filter may cause it to fail another — which tradeoffs am I making?
