# Lens #50 — Parallelism

Multiple simultaneous sub-puzzles prevent dead ends. When stuck on one, players work on another. Partial progress is always available — eliminating the "I'm stuck, I quit" response. (s005, ch.12)

## Core Questions

> When players are stuck, do they have other things to work on? Is there a way to give players multiple parallel challenges?
