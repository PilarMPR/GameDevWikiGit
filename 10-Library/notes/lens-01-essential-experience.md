# Lens #1 — Essential Experience

Stop thinking about the game; think about the player's experience. Every design decision should serve the essential experience you want to create. (s005, ch.2)

## Core Questions

> What experience do I want the player to have? What is essential to that experience? How can my game capture that essence?
