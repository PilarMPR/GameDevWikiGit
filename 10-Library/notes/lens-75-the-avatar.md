# Lens #75 — The Avatar

The avatar should invite player identification — a balance of "me" (enough blank slate to project into) and "not me" (distinct enough to be interesting as a character). (s005, ch.18)

## Core Questions

> Does the avatar invite identification? Is there the right mix of blank slate and distinct personality? Does the player feel like they ARE the avatar?
