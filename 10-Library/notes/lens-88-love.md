# Lens #88 — Love

The ultimate engagement metric: does the player love the game? Do they talk about it? Think about it when they're not playing? Return to it repeatedly? (s005, ch.19)

## Core Questions

> Does the player love this game? Do I love this game? What would it take for players to love it?
