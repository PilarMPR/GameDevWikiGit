# Lens #23 — Emergence

Emergence = unexpected, interesting strategies arising from the rules. More verbs × more objects × more subjects = more emergence. High resultant-to-operative ratio = depth without added content. (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> How many verbs do players have? How many objects do they interact with? Do surprising and interesting situations arise within the rules? Are there dominant strategies?
