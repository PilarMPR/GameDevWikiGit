# Lens #91 — Playtesting

Playtesting with the right audience at the right stage is the only reliable quality signal. Playtest observations must actually change the design or the playtesting is theater. (s005, ch.25)

## Core Questions

> Are we testing with the right audience? At the right development stage? Are playtest results actually informing design decisions?
