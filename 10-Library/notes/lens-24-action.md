# Lens #24 — Action

Operative actions (what the designer creates) vs. resultant actions (what emerges from their combinations). The gap between them is where gameplay lives. (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> What are the operative actions in my game? What resultant actions do players discover? Are the resultant actions as interesting as the operative ones?
