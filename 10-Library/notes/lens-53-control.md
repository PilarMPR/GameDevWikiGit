# Lens #53 — Control

Players must feel in control. When the interface doesn't do what they expect, players feel cheated rather than challenged. Clear control = the player can execute their intent reliably. (s005, ch.13)

## Core Questions

> When players use the interface, does it do what they expect? Do they feel in control? Are there moments when the interface gets in the way of intent?
