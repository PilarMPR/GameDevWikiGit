# Lens #83 — The Nameless Quality

Christopher Alexander's "quality without a name" — a living, whole quality that certain environments and games possess but cannot be fully defined. You know it when you feel it. (s005, ch.18)

## Core Questions

> Does the world have a quality beyond description — something that draws players in and makes them feel whole in the game's presence?
