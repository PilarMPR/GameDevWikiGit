# Lens #71 — Freedom

Players want the *feeling* of freedom, not necessarily unlimited freedom. Appropriate constraints that feel natural produce the best of both worlds: designed experiences that feel freely explored. (s005, ch.16)

## Core Questions

> Do players have real freedom or just the feeling of freedom? Are the constraints natural-feeling or arbitrary? How can I give more freedom while maintaining the designed experience?
