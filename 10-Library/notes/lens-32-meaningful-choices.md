# Lens #32 — Meaningful Choices

Choices = Desires = freedom. Choices > Desires = overwhelmed. Choices < Desires = frustrated. Non-dominated meaningful choices define game depth. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Are my choices meaningful? Are there too many or too few? Are any dominated (one option always better)? Do players feel free and in control?
