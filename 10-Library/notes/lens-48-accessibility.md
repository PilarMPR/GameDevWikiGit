# Lens #48 — Accessibility

Puzzles must communicate their goal clearly from the start and invite players to engage. If the player doesn't know what they're trying to do, they won't engage. (s005, ch.12)

## Core Questions

> Is the goal immediately clear? Does the game draw players in and make them want to interact? What is the first thing a new player will try to do?
