# Lens #6 — Problem Solving

Games are problem-solving activities approached with a playful attitude. Every game poses problems for the player to solve; the quality of the problems determines the quality of the experience. (s005, ch.3)

## Core Questions

> What problems does my game ask the player to solve? Are those problems interesting? How are new problems revealed?
