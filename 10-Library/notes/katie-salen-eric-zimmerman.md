# Katie Salen & Eric Zimmerman

Game designers and academics. Co-authors of *Rules of Play: Game Design Fundamentals* (s013) — the foundational academic text for game studies.

## Key ideas

- **Meaningful play** — emerges when player action and system outcome are discernable and integrated (s013).
- **Game definition** — "a system in which players engage in artificial conflict, defined by rules, that results in a quantifiable outcome" (s013).
- **Three schemas** — Rules (formal), Play (experiential), Culture (cultural) — all three must be analyzed for full understanding (s013).
- **Magic circle** — the bounded space of play, where game rules apply and special meanings accrue (s013, ch.9).
