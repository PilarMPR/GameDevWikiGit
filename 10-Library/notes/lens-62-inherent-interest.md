# Lens #62 — Inherent Interest

Some things are inherently interesting (dangerous things, powerful characters, important events) while others are not. The most compelling experiences combine inherently interesting elements with novel combinations. (s005, ch.14)

## Core Questions

> What is inherently interesting about my experience? What makes players curious about the next moment? How can I increase inherent interest?
