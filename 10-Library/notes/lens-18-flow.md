# Lens #18 — Flow

The flow channel is the narrow band where challenge matches skill. Maintain it through clear goals, direct feedback, no distractions, and continuously escalating challenge. Flow is hard to test — requires long observation; watch for boredom and frustration exits. (s005, ch.9)

## Core Questions

> Are goals clear? Does the challenge steadily escalate with improving skill? Are there distractions? Is feedback tight enough? Where do players leave the flow channel?
