# Lens #98 — Responsibility

Designers are responsible for the experiences they create and their effects on players and society. Ask whether you'd be comfortable with this game's full impact if it became public knowledge. (s005, ch.32)

## Core Questions

> Am I willing to be publicly responsible for this game? Does it reflect my values? What is the worst potential effect? Would I be proud of this game in twenty years?
