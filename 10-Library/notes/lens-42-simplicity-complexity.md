# Lens #42 — Simplicity/Complexity

Innate complexity (complex rules) vs. emergent complexity (simple rules producing complex situations). Prefer emergent complexity. Every rule that isn't pulling its weight should be removed. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> What are the most complex parts of my game? Is that complexity innate or emergent? Is the complexity worth it? What could I remove without losing important experiences?
