# Lens #30 — Fairness

Symmetric games are fair by definition; asymmetric games must be deliberately balanced. Perceived fairness matters as much as mathematical fairness — players quit games they feel are unfair. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Is my game symmetric or asymmetric? If asymmetric, is it balanced? How do I know it is fair? Do players feel it is fair?
