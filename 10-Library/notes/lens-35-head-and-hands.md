# Lens #35 — Head and Hands

Physical skill (reflexes, speed, accuracy) vs. mental skill (strategy, planning, memory). Both can coexist. The mix must match the game's design promise and be communicated clearly to the player. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Does my game require physical skill, mental skill, or both? Is the balance appropriate for my intended audience? Does the game communicate which type of challenge it offers?
