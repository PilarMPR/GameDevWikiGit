# Lens #29 — Chance

Randomness creates uncertainty and variety. Well-designed chance feels fair (the probability distribution is appropriate for the audience and genre) and keeps the skill-to-chance ratio tuned to the game's goals. (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> What random elements exist? Do they feel fair? Is the skill-to-chance ratio right for the audience? Does luck produce interesting outcomes or just frustrating ones?
