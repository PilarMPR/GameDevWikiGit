# Lens #86 — Community

Games that support community formation outlast games that don't. Communities create ongoing reasons to return, even after the core mechanics have been mastered. (s005, ch.19)

## Core Questions

> Does the game support community formation around it? Do players have reasons to return beyond personal achievement? Is there a social fabric the game creates?
