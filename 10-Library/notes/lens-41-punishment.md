# Lens #41 — Punishment

Punishment well-designed increases enjoyment by creating stakes and making success meaningful. It must be proportional, for things players can understand and prevent, and convertible to reward wherever possible. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> What punishments exist? Are they fair? Are they proportional? Do they help players improve? Can any punishments be converted into rewards? Is there a fear of failure?
