# Lens #87 — Griefing

Some players will deliberately harm others' enjoyment. Design systems that either prevent griefing, make it unproductive, or convert antisocial behavior into acceptable game conflict. (s005, ch.19)

## Core Questions

> Are there ways players can harm other players' experience? How is antisocial behavior handled? Are there mechanics that make griefing feel like a loss for the griefer?
