# Lens #20 — Judgment

Players judge themselves through games. Design so they feel good about themselves when they succeed — competent, capable, and appropriately challenged. (s005, ch.9)

## Core Questions

> How does the game judge the player's performance? Does the player feel judged fairly? Does the game make players feel good or bad about themselves? Do players feel competent?
