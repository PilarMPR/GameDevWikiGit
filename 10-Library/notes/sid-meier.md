# Sid Meier

Legendary game designer. Creator of Civilization, Pirates!, Railroad Tycoon, Alpha Centauri. Co-founder of Firaxis Games.

## Key ideas

- **"A game is a series of interesting decisions"** — one of the most quoted game design statements. Reframes game design from systems or stories to the moment-to-moment decision quality.
- Complexity emerging from simple, interacting systems — the Civilization series exemplifies emergence from simple rules.
- Cited by Sellers (s001) on "interesting decisions" and by Schell as an influence.
