# Lens #85 — Expression

Players want to express who they are through games — their playstyle, their character, their choices. Expression is MDA aesthetic #7. Design systems that let players show themselves meaningfully. (s005, ch.19)

## Core Questions

> Does the game let players express who they are? Are there sufficient expressive options? Are the expressions meaningful (visible to others, consequential to the game)?
