# Lens #80 — Status

Status is a primary human social motivator. Characters who clearly have high or low status create compelling social dynamics. Status transactions (gaining/losing status) are inherently dramatic. (s005, ch.18)

## Core Questions

> What is each character's status? Are status transactions dramatically interesting? How does status change across the experience?
