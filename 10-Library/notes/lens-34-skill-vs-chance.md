# Lens #34 — Skill vs. Chance

More chance = relaxed, casual, less measure of skill. More skill = competitive, serious, measure of excellence. The ratio must match the intended audience and emotional tone. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Is the balance of skill and chance appropriate for my intended audience? Does randomness add tension and uncertainty, or just frustrate? Would the game be better with more or less randomness?
