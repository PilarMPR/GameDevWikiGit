# Tracy Fullerton

Game designer, academic, director of USC Games, founder of USC Game Innovation Lab. Author of *Game Design Workshop* (s008) — the most widely used undergraduate game design textbook.

## Key ideas

- **Playcentric design** — center everything on the player's experience, assessed through continuous playtesting with real players (s008).
- **Formal + dramatic + dynamic elements** — three layers: structural skeleton (formal), experiential layer (dramatic), play in motion (dynamic) (s008).
- **Iterative method** — conceptualize → prototype → playtest → evaluate → repeat. Physical prototypes first (s008).
- **Playtesting is not QA** — it's learning: "can they play?" vs. "are they having the right experience?" are different activities (s008).
