# Lens #59 — Channels and Dimensions

Different feedback channels (visual, audio, haptic) have different properties and cognitive loads. Multi-channel feedback is more reliable. Overloaded channels produce noise; underused channels are missed opportunities. (s005, ch.13)

## Core Questions

> Am I using all available feedback channels thoughtfully? Are any channels overloaded? Are any underused? Do different dimensions of information use distinct channels?
