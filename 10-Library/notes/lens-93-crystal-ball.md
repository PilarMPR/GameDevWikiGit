# Lens #93 — Crystal Ball

Design for the world as it will be when the game ships, not as it is today. Technology and player expectations shift; games in long development must anticipate these shifts. (s005, ch.27)

## Core Questions

> What will the gaming landscape look like when this ships? Are we designing for the present or for the future? What trends could change the context of our game?
