# Lens #56 — Transparency

Players should never be surprised by hidden interface behavior. Everything the player needs to know should be visible or discoverable. Opacity produces frustration and the perception of unfairness. (s005, ch.13)

## Core Questions

> Is the interface invisible when working well? Are there hidden states that ever surprise the player? Is all important information visible?
