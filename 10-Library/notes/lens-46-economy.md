# Lens #46 — Economy

The game's resource economy determines fairness, progression pacing, and strategic depth. Resources must flow in and out at appropriate rates. Balanced economies create sustainable engagement. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Is the game's economy properly balanced? Are resource rates appropriate? Are there sinks and sources for all resources? Does the economy feel fair to all player types?
