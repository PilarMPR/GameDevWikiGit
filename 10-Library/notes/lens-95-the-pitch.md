# Lens #95 — The Pitch

The one-sentence pitch is the most compressed form of the design vision. A compelling pitch communicates the essential experience immediately; a weak pitch suggests the design is unclear. (s005, ch.29)

## Core Questions

> What is the one-sentence description of my game? Is it compelling? Does it accurately represent the experience?
