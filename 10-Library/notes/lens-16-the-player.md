# Lens #16 — The Player

Think about the player in everything you do. Know their demographics and psychographics; be their advocate. Watch them play rather than just imagining them. (s005, ch.8)

## Core Questions

> Am I thinking about the player in everything I do? Do I truly understand their demographics and psychographics? What would I want if I were in their place?
