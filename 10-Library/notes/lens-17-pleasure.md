# Lens #17 — Pleasure

List the specific pleasures your game delivers from LeBlanc's 8 aesthetics: Sensation, Fantasy, Narrative, Challenge, Fellowship, Discovery, Expression, Submission. Design deliberately toward the intended pleasures. (s005, ch.8)

## Core Questions

> What pleasures does my game create? Are they the right pleasures for my audience? Are there pleasures I could add that would strengthen the experience?
