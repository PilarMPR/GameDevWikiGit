# Lens #70 — Story

The story of a game should emerge primarily through play, not through cutscenes. Story felt through interaction resonates more deeply than story merely watched. (s005, ch.15)

## Core Questions

> Does my game tell a good story? Is the story told through play or just delivered through cutscenes? Does the story emerge naturally from gameplay?
