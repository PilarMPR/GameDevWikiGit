# Lens #22 — Dynamic State

Objects are nouns; attributes and states are adjectives. Track the complete state of all game objects. Design information asymmetry deliberately — who knows what determines the game's social and strategic character. (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> What are all the objects in my game? Their attributes? Their states and the transitions between them? What is known by the game only / all players / some players? Would changing this alter the game positively?
