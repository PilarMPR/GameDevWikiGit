# Lens #2 — Surprise

Surprise is fundamental to fun. The brain is hardwired to value unpredictable positive outcomes over predictable ones. Games must generate novelty to sustain engagement. (s005, ch.3)

## Core Questions

> What will surprise players? Does the story, rules, and art generate novel surprises? Do the rules let players surprise each other and themselves?
