# Lens #4 — Curiosity

Curiosity — the desire to resolve open questions — is a powerful motivator. The game should constantly generate questions in the player's mind and reward curiosity with meaningful answers. (s005, ch.3)

## Core Questions

> What questions does my game make the player ask? Does my game reward curiosity with satisfying answers?
