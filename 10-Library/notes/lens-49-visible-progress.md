# Lens #49 — Visible Progress

Players must be able to see incremental progress toward their goal. A riddle demands binary knowledge; a puzzle lets you see yourself approach the solution. Visible progress sustains motivation. (s005, ch.12)

## Core Questions

> Can players see that they are making progress? Are interim steps of success available? Does the game feel like it is solvable because players can see progress?
