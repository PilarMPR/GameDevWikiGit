# Lens #19 — Needs

Games fulfill human psychological needs. Know which need your game promises to fulfill, and verify through playtesting that it actually delivers. Unmet promises produce particularly bitter disappointment. (s005, ch.9)

## Core Questions

> What needs does my game fulfill? Does it deliver on its promise? Am I designing a game that truly fulfills those needs?
