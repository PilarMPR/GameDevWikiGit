# Ernest Adams

Game designer, academic, author. Author of *Fundamentals of Game Design* (s006).

## Key ideas

- **Player-centric design** — the designer is the player's advocate; define target audience and player experience first (s006).
- **Five mechanic families** — physics, internal economy, progression, tactical maneuvering, social interaction (s006, ch.14).
- **Gameplay = hierarchy of challenges** — the structural backbone of player engagement (s006, ch.13).
- **Core mechanics** — the rules and data that implement gameplay (s006, ch.14).
