# Lens #94 — The Client

Client wants vs. client needs are not the same. Deliver what the client needs to succeed (the underlying goal), not necessarily what they asked for. This requires deep listening to understand the real goal. (s005, ch.27)

## Core Questions

> Who is the client? What do they want? What do they actually need? How do I satisfy both?
