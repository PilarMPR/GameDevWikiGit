# Lens #64 — Projection

Players project themselves into the game world. The more the world invites imagination and projection — leaving space for the player's own experience — the more invested they become. (s005, ch.14)

## Core Questions

> Does the world invite imagination and projection? Does the player feel ownership of their character? Do players feel like they are in the world?
