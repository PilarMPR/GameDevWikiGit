# Nicole Lazzaro

UX researcher and founder of XEODesign. Known for the "Four Keys to Fun" framework (not captured as a source in this wiki). Her emotional state research (from playtesting observations) identified Hard Fun, Easy Fun, Altered States, and People Fun as the four emotional contexts of gaming.

## Key ideas

- **Four Keys to Fun:** Hard Fun (fiero — challenge/mastery), Easy Fun (curiosity/exploration), Altered States (immersion), People Fun (social/amusement). Complementary to MDA's aesthetics taxonomy.
- Her research informed many F2P design practices (session pacing, difficulty, social hooks).
