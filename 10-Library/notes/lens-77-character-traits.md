# Lens #77 — Character Traits

Character traits must be consistent — the same trait expressed in different contexts makes a character feel real. Traits described but not demonstrated through action feel false. (s005, ch.18)

## Core Questions

> What are the traits of each character? Are those traits consistent? Are they reflected in actions, not just descriptions?
