# Lens #58 — Juiciness

Every interaction should be satisfying. Small touches (sounds, animations, particles, screen shake) that reward incidental interaction create a pleasurable kinesthetic layer. "Juice" makes good mechanics feel great. (s005, ch.13)

## Core Questions

> Is every interaction satisfying? Are there small touches that reward incidental interaction? Is there enough juice (audio/visual/haptic feedback) for every action?
