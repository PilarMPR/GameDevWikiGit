# Lens #74 — The World

A consistent, interesting world invites exploration and builds emotional investment. The world must have a coherent tone, follow its own internal rules, and reward curiosity with discovery. (s005, ch.17)

## Core Questions

> Is the game world interesting to explore? Is it internally consistent? Does it have a coherent tone? Does it make the player curious?
