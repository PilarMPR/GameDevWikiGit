# Lens #100 — Your Secret Purpose

Why are you making this game? What does it mean to you personally? Have you found a way to put that meaning into the game? The secret purpose is what makes a game resonate beyond its mechanics. (s005, ch.32)

## Core Questions

> Why am I making this game? What does it mean to me? Have I found a way to express that meaning in the game? Is the game truly my own?
