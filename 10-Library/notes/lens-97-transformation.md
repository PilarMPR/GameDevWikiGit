# Lens #97 — Transformation

The most powerful games change the people who play them — in skills, perspectives, relationships, or self-understanding. Design for transformation, not just entertainment. (s005, ch.32)

## Core Questions

> Does this game transform the player? Is that transformation positive? What would it take for the game to genuinely change someone?
