# Lens #8 — Holographic Design

Think about the experience and the game simultaneously — like a doctor seeing both skin and skeleton. Every element should serve both the game's structure and the player's experience. (s005, ch.4)

## Core Questions

> Can every element of my game be explained in terms of the core experience I am trying to create? Can I look at my game from the perspective of both the game and the experience?
