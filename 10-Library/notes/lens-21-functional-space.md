# Lens #21 — Functional Space

The abstract topology of play: discrete vs. continuous, dimensions, bounded and connected areas. The magic circle as geometry. Functional space is independent of aesthetic presentation. (s005, ch.10)

## Core Questions

> Is the space discrete or continuous? How many dimensions does it have? What are the boundaries and how are they determined? Are there connected sub-spaces? Is there more than one useful abstraction of the space?
