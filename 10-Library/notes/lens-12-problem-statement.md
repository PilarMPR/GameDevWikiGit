# Lens #12 — Problem Statement

Good design begins with a clear problem statement naming both goal and constraints. This gives a creative space, a measurement criterion, and shared vocabulary for the team. (s005, ch.6)

## Core Questions

> Can I clearly state the problem I am trying to solve? What are the unspoken assumptions? Is a game even the best solution?
