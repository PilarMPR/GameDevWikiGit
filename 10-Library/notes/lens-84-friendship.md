# Lens #84 — Friendship

Games that bring people together as friends satisfy deeper needs than games played alone. The conversations after the game are as important as the game itself. (s005, ch.19)

## Core Questions

> Does the game bring people together as friends? What do players talk about after playing? Does the game create shared experiences that strengthen relationships?
