# Lens #31 — Challenge

The challenge must be appropriate and should scale. Keep all skill levels engaged — too easy for experts, too hard for novices both break the experience. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> What is the challenge? Is it well-calibrated for the audience? Does challenge scale with player skill? Is there enough challenge for skilled players? Enough success for novices?
