# Lens #40 — Reward

8 types: praise, points, prolonged play, portals (new content), spectacle, expression, power, resources, and completeness. Variable rewards sustain engagement better than fixed ones. Rewards should grow gradually with progression. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> What rewards exist in my game? Are they the right types for my audience? When do they arrive? Are they meaningful? Are variable rewards present? Do rewards feel earned?
