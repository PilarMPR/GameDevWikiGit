# Lens #44 — Character

Games can have personality — distinctive quirks and "lovable flaws" that make them memorable and personal. Elegance without character produces something perfect but soulless. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Does my game have distinctive and unique character? What makes my game feel like it couldn't have been made by anyone else?
