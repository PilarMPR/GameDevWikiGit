# Lens #36 — Competition

Competition taps a primal human drive to establish status and demonstrate mastery. Design competition so players genuinely want to win — for the right reasons. (s005, ch.11)

## Core Questions

> Does my game make players want to win? Is the competition level appropriate? How does it feel to lose — crushing or motivating? Is winning meaningful?
