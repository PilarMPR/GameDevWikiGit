# s003 · The Hows and Whys of Level Design (De Jong)

**TL;DR** — A practitioner's primer on level design as a discipline distinct from game design: the craft of building spaces and moment-to-moment experiences players move through. Emphasizes gameplay-first layout before aesthetics (s003, Preface).
**Provenance caveats** — Summarized from extracted text; TOC not cleanly machine-readable.

## Key ideas

- **Game design vs. level design**: level designers work within given mechanics to shape pacing, flow, difficulty, and mood (s003).
- **Gameplay-first** layout — readability, guiding the player, sightlines, pacing of tension/release before aesthetics (s003).
- **Mood and atmosphere** via lighting, composition, and audio as deliberate emotional tools (s003).
