# s006 · Fundamentals of Game Design (Adams)

**TL;DR** — A comprehensive textbook built around player-centric design: the designer is an advocate for the player, defining a clear target audience and player experience before mechanics. Combines a general design process with deep dives into craft areas and major genres (s006, ch.2, ch.4).
**Provenance caveats** — Verified TOC + chapter summaries.

## Key ideas

- **Player-centric design** & target audience as anchor of every decision (s006, ch.2, 4).
- **VandenBerghe's Five Domains of Play** for player psychology (s006, ch.4).
- **Gameplay = hierarchy of challenges**; skill, stress & absolute difficulty (s006, ch.13).
- **Core mechanics** = the rules/data that implement gameplay; clean separation of gameplay (challenges + actions) from mechanics (s006, ch.13–14).
- **Creating the User Experience** (ch.12) — player-centric UI, interaction & camera models, accessibility.
