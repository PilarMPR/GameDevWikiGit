# Schell's 100 Lenses — by Chapter

All 100 lenses from *The Art of Game Design* (s005), sorted by lens number. Each links to its atomic note.

> **How to use:** apply any lens as a focusing question on your current design. Consider lenses in groups by the chapter that introduced them — they're designed to build on each other.

TABLE lens, title, disciplines FROM "10-Library/notes"
WHERE lens >= 1 AND lens <= 100
